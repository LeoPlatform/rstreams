RStreams === Rockin’ Streams

AWS-native event streaming and messaging platform

A light abstraction on AWS Kinesis, S3 and DynamoDB to provide an event-streaming platform with lambda-based event handlers that supports near real-time 1 to many, many to 1 and fanout at massive scale: billions of events a day.
